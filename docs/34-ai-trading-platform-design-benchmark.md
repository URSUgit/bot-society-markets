# BITprivat AI Trading Platform Design Benchmark

Version 1.0 - May 2026

## Decision

The best fit to benchmark for BITprivat is **TrendSpider**, not because we should clone it visually, but because its product model is closest to what BITprivat is becoming:

- AI market assistant
- strategy testing
- no-code AI strategy training
- scanners and pattern discovery
- alerts and bots
- chart-first research workflow

BITprivat should use this benchmark as a product-quality target while keeping its own original brand, layout, legal controls, and copy-trading/social-trader focus.

## Competitive Pattern Map

| Platform | What To Learn | What BITprivat Should Do Differently |
|---|---|---|
| TrendSpider | AI strategy workflow, no-code strategy testing, alerts, bots, chart automation | Add social-trader evidence, paper allocation, and legal gates before live automation. |
| TradingView | Chart-first layout, alerts, screeners, multi-chart workflows, global market coverage | Build a simpler cockpit for retail users and expose creator-bot reasoning beside the chart. |
| Trade Ideas | Real-time scanning, AI-generated entry/exit ideas, day-trader alert cadence | Apply scanner logic to crypto, prediction markets, creator evidence, and paper bots. |
| Tickeron | AI robot marketplace with performance metrics and sorting | Make ROI provenance transparent and label proxy ROI versus price-validated ROI. |

## UX Direction For BITprivat

BITprivat should become an **AI trading cockpit**:

1. **Chart-first center**: the chart and P&L are the visual anchor.
2. **AI analyst always visible**: the user should see what the bot is thinking and why.
3. **Scanner cadence**: market, social, and venue signals should feel live and ranked.
4. **Strategy before automation**: every bot should have a path from evidence to backtest to paper deployment.
5. **Risk-gated execution**: paper-first by default; live trading requires legal, suitability, venue, allocation, and kill-switch gates.
6. **Performance marketplace**: creator bots and AI bots should be browsable as compact cards with score, risk, ROI window, evidence count, and deploy/follow actions.

## Implemented UI Change

The dashboard hero was redesigned as a compact AI trading cockpit inspired by the best patterns above:

- left side: clear product promise and primary actions
- right side: AI desk panel with chart motif, scanner, analyst, strategy lab, and risk gate
- window size increased for a more platform-like workspace
- dashboard asset version bumped to `ai-cockpit-1`
